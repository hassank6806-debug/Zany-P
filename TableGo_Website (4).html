How It Works

Ready in 3 simple steps

No tools, no setup time. PorTable mounts and dismounts in seconds so it's always ready when you need it.

1

Loop & Attach

Loop the headrest strap around the back-seat headrest and pull snug. The buckle locks it securely in under 10 seconds.

2

Unfold & Adjust

Open the board flat. Position it at a comfortable angle for eating, studying, or using a laptop — it sits stable without wobbling.

3

Use & Fold Away

Enjoy your meal, finish your work. When done, fold flat and slide behind the seat. Easy, clean, and always ready for next time.
